I decided to keep my regression and visual simple, because I believe I set up my month variable to be able to differentiate between pre and post introduction of Scorecard data. I was able to filter my data set down to show data only after the introduction of the Scorecard data (Sept. 2015). This allowed me to conclude that the interest between high and low earning colleges really didn’t change much after the introduction of the Scorecard data.

So, we should can conclude that the introduction of the College Scorecard slightly decreased interest in Low Earning colleges by 0.001517 units relative to what it did for High Earning colleges, with a s.e. of 0.000051. These results comes from the month coefficient in my regression.
